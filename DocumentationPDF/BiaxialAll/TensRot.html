BIAXIALALL` SYMBOL             

TensRot

 TensRot[tensor,EulerAngles]  Rotates the dielectric tensor through a coordinate rotation given by the Three element vector EulerAngles.  First about the z-axis then about the y axis and then about the z-axis again.
