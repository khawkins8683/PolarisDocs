CleanUpMMdata

 CleanUpMMdata[imagedat_,rotval_:0] used to remove depolarization from the measured Mueller matrix (because our routines don't account for it).  Routine performes a polar decomposition (Depolarizer x Retarder x Diattenuator) and remultiplies the retarder and diattenuator Mueller matrix images to get a non-depolarizing Mueller matrix.  Additonally a uniform coordinate rotation can be applied (sometimes necessary with reflection data, try rotval of 1.6°)
