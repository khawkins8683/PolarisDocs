Retfit

 Retfit[θ_,a_:.014,l2_:.000,b_:20.5*10^-5,c_:-0.00000007] is a polynomial of the form a+l2*(θ*41.5/33°)+b*(θ*41.5/33°)^2+c*(θ*41.5/33°)^4.  Pretty ad-hoc, but it kinda works
